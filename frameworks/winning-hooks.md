## Controversial / Pattern-Interrupt

**Example:** I just cancelled ALL our employee benefits. They were a total waste of money.

**Template:** `I [took a controversial action]. [Briefly explain why in a way that sounds inflammatory].`

---

**Example:** I landed a $20K project one month after becoming a copywriter.

**Template:** `I landed a [high-value opportunity] just [short time] after [starting in my field/role]. Here's exactly what I did (X steps), opposite to most [role/profession]:`

---

## Objection-Handling

**Example:** "I can find someone who will do this for $5 per 1,000 words!"

**Template:** `"[Someone or competitor] can do this [for a lower price/in less time]!" Yeah, and I can find [absurd comparison] - that doesn't signify high quality, [Name].`

## Shocking

**Example:** The late CEO of Zappos, Tony Hsieh, used to offer new hires $2,000 on the spot to leave the company.

**Template:** `The [role/title] of [well-known person/company] used to [surprising or unconventional practice]. Highly unusual but [explain the purpose or effect of the practice].`

---

**Example:** My boss stalked me into my current job.

**Template:** `My [colleague/peer/company] [took surprising action that led to specific outcome]. [Share where it started - give brief details on any significant events that led up to your surprising experience].`

---

**Example:** I once quoted a company $18,000 for a rebrand.

**Template:** `I once [took a standard action] for a [service/solution]. [Briefly describe unexpected reaction or result]. I felt [emotional reaction]. Here's how it played out.`

---

## Victory

**Example:** In 2003 LEGO was $800m in debt. But by 2015 they had $1billion+ in sales. What was at the heart of such a crazy comeback?

**Template:** `In [year], [entity/individual] faced [challenging situation]. By [later year], they achieved [extraordinary turnaround/result]. How did they do it? [Core concept or principle].`

---

**Example:** MAJOR NEWS

**Template:** `HUGE NEWS: [EXCITING ANNOUNCEMENT]. I just [significant achievement], [description of the achievement].`

---

**Example:** My first 6 months writing online, I made $0.

**Template:** `My first [time period] doing [activity], I [achieved very little/nothing]. The next [time period] -> [small milestone]. The next [time period] -> [bigger milestone]. By the end of [larger timeframe] -> [massive milestone]. But I could've done it quicker. Here are the X mistakes I'd avoid (if starting over from scratch):`

**Template:** `Today is one of the [adjective] moments of my [specific pursuit]. After [time period] of [effortful endeavours]... After countless [specific challenges]... After seeing [impressive outcomes]... I've finally [achieved milestone/created something].`
